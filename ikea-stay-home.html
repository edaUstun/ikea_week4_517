IKEA “Stay Home” : The Power of Empathy in Digital Marketing

Campaign Summary

At the beginning of 2020, the Covid-19 pandemic affected the entire world, and everyone was forced to stay at home. IKEA was affected by this situation and shared its “Stay Home” digital campaign on its Instagram accounts with the whole world to motivate everyone. With this campaign, IKEA aimed to remind people that “home” is a safe place, going beyond simply increasing sales. During this challenging period, IKEA focused on sharing messages that brought comfort, health, and peace of mind to people, rather than prioritizing sales.

I2M Matrix Analysis

1. Website

The “Stay Home” campaign was only shared on social media platforms. The campaign reached wide audiences through sincere and creative content shared on Instagram (IKEA, 2020). It was not featured on websites. As a result, customers were unable to obtain information about the campaign from the website. Although the number of views on the IKEA website increased during the Covid-19 pandemic, many people were unable to find out about the campaign because it was not published on the campaign website.

2. E-mail

IKEA prepared catalogs about the campaign and sent them to some countries via e-newsletters. The catalogs contained games for people to have fun at home without getting bored.

3. Search Engines

It appears that no clear SEO or SEM strategy was followed during the campaign. It was noticed that IKEA's “stay home” content was triggered less by users than by the brand itself in Google searches.

4. Online Advertising

IKEA aimed to make the campaign naturally accessible to users. This may have supported the brand's empathetic approach while limiting digital access. However, this choice prevented the campaign from being openly accessible on digital platforms. One of the digital platforms, the Google Display Network, did not publish this ad. The success of digital marketing campaigns depends on the effective use of appropriate communication channels (Strauss & Frost, 2014).

5. Sales Promotions

IKEA has not conducted any discount or promotional activities during this campaign. With this communication approach, IKEA has demonstrated a value-oriented approach to its users.

6. Public Relations

IKEA's campaign during the pandemic was described by the press as “exemplary brand behavior.” Powerful brands such as FastCompany and AdWeek praised IKEA for this campaign. As a result, IKEA gained more value and prestige among consumers through this campaign. According to Fast Company (2020), IKEA’s campaign conveyed the stay-at-home message warmly and effectively during the pandemic.

7. News

With this campaign, IKEA took an approach focused on empathy rather than sales, which easily earned it coverage in many international news outlets.

8. Word of Mouth

With the increase in social media usage during the Covid period, the campaign published on Instagram was liked by users and reposted, creating a natural advertising network.

9. Popular Culture

IKEA only shared this campaign on Instagram and did not sign any advertising agreements with influencers or celebrities. This may be the reason why the campaign did not go completely viral.

10. Gamification

Users staying at home launched challenges on social media to make their time more enjoyable. IKEA could have gained more engagement on its page by launching similar stay-at-home challenges.

Strengths and Weaknesses of the Campaign

  • Strength Point: A non-sales-based campaign fostered empathy and trust among users.
  • Strength Point: Even without any social media sharing agreements, the campaign was able to generate high likes and shares on social media.
  • Weaknesses Point: It was not featured on the campaign website, and gamification was not used to establish a deeper connection with users.
  • Weaknesses Point: No agreement has been reached with any influencer or television media to generate more engagement.

Suggestions for Improvement

  1. To increase web engagement, a competition could be organized to evaluate photos taken by users under the “stay at home” campaign.
  2. Photos or videos shared on social media with the hashtag “#stayathome” could be reposted on IKEA's Instagram page to show users that they are visible and valued.
  3. The campaign could have achieved full viral success by signing advertising agreements with celebrities or individuals with brand value.

My Personal Anecdote

During the pandemic, I had the opportunity to discover this campaign on social media. Many people were sharing the safe and peaceful moments they had created in their own homes through this campaign. By reading the text they wrote under the shared photos, I better understood that everyone was sharing the same process in the same way. I didn't make any purchases through this campaign, but I felt more empathy towards many people.
